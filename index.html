For Students, By Students.

PeduL is a non-profit higher education crowd funding platform dedicated to alleviating the financial burdens of college. PeduL facilitates money directly from campaigns to universities in the form of a third-party scholarship.

A venture by college students, for college students. This matter is not just business, it is personal.

For Students, By Students. This is personal.
